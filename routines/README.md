# Routines

Daily, weekly, and monthly habits. What you're trying to do consistently. What's actually working vs what's aspirational.

## Suggested files
- `daily.md` -- what you try to do every day
- `weekly.md` -- weekly cadence
- `monthly.md` -- monthly check-ins
- `morning.md` / `evening.md` -- specific routines for parts of the day
- `working.md` -- routines that are sticking
- `not-working.md` -- routines you tried that didn't stick (and why)

## Pushback rule
If you list a routine here and the daily logs show you're not actually doing it, I should call it out. Aspirational routines that never become real are noise -- either commit or kill them.
