## Honest pushback (per CLAUDE.md rules)
You have a lot of "set up but not built" SaaS ideas right now. ClipMeta is the only one with paying users. Before investing real time in Beekeeper Hive Manager, I'd ask:

1. **Why this niche specifically?** Do you know beekeepers? Have you talked to any?
2. **What's the wedge?** What does an existing beekeeper hive manager NOT do that yours would?
3. **What does Phase 0 validation look like?** Same as [[site_rules|SiteRules]] — talk to 5 beekeepers BEFORE building anything.
4. **Is this realistic for someone in [[life_state|North Port, FL]]?** Florida has beekeepers but the network is harder to find than fence contractors.

If you have answers to those, this becomes worth working on. If you don't, it's a fun idea that should stay an idea until you talk to a real beekeeper.
