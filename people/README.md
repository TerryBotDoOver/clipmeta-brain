# People

Notes on the people in Levi's life.

One file per person, named like `firstname-lastname.md` or `firstname.md` for unique first names.

## What goes in each file
- How you know them (relationship, how you met, when)
- Birthday / important dates
- Key facts about them (kids, partner, job, where they live)
- Things they care about / are working on
- Recent conversations or interactions worth remembering
- Things you've promised them
- Their preferences (food, communication style, anything specific)
- Things they're sensitive about

## Why this exists
So I can remind you of things like "haven't talked to your sister in 3 weeks" or "Sarah mentioned her dad was sick last month, ask how he's doing" -- the small things that humans forget but matter.
