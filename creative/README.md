# Creative

Drone footage projects, video production, creative ideas, anything making.

## Suggested files
- `drone-projects/` -- one file per project (location, goals, footage captured, status)
- `video-projects/` -- video editing projects, ideas, scripts
- `gear.md` -- equipment you own and notes about it
- `ideas.md` -- raw creative ideas to revisit
- `inspiration.md` -- references, things that inspired you

## Why this is separate from business/clipmeta/
ClipMeta is a SaaS product. Drone footage is your creative practice. They're related (ClipMeta exists because of your creative work) but they're not the same thing. Keep them separate so the brain doesn't conflate "creating footage" with "running a software business."
