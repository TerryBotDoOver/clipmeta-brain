# Finance

High-level financial goals, decisions, and tracking. NOT account numbers, NOT passwords, NOT actual balances if you want to be safe.

## What's safe to put here
- Monthly burn rate (rough)
- Savings goals
- Big financial decisions you're weighing
- Subscriptions you're paying for
- Income sources
- Tax notes
- Long-term goals (buy a house, X in savings, etc.)

## What goes in private/ instead
- Account numbers
- Passwords
- Specific balances
- Tax IDs
- Anything you wouldn't want a stranger to read

## Why I exist for this
Money is one area where humans are bad at long-term thinking. I can hold the long-term picture and remind you when short-term decisions don't match it.
